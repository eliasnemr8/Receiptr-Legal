Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Summary: Receiptr stores your financial data on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not operate our own servers to store your financial data. We do not sell your data. We do not advertise to you. Receipt images and budget setup information are processed by Anthropic's AI and are not retained beyond the processing request.

1. Who We Are

Receiptr ("we", "us", "our") is a personal expense tracking application developed and operated as an independent app. For privacy inquiries, you can contact us at eliasnemr7@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account information (registered users only):

  • Name and email address provided at signup
  • Account creation date and time

Financial data you enter or scan:

  • Merchant names, transaction amounts, dates, and categories
  • Receipt images you choose to scan
  • Individual line items from receipts
  • Transaction notes you add

Budget and savings information:

  • Budget limits you set per category
  • Savings goals, target amounts, and target dates
  • Recurring transaction schedules you configure

AI Budget Setup information (if you use this feature):

  • Monthly income range
  • City and country of residence
  • Household size
  • Housing situation and estimated costs
  • Transportation method and estimated costs
  • Dining habits
  • Whether you have children or pets and estimated costs
  • Savings goals

App preferences:

  • Currency selection
  • Custom categories you create
  • Notification and budget reset settings
  • Face ID / biometric lock preference

What we do NOT collect:

  • Bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or any payment credentials
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Browsing history or behavior outside the app
  • Contacts, photos (beyond what you explicitly choose to scan), or other device data

3. How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used solely to provide the Receiptr service to you:

  • To display your transaction history, spending summaries, and budget progress
  • To sync your data across your devices via your personal iCloud account
  • To process receipt images through AI to extract transaction details
  • To generate a personalized budget through AI based on your financial situation
  • To deliver local budget alert notifications on your device
  • To allow you to export your data as a CSV file

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the app's features to you.

4. AI Processing — Receipt Scanning

When you scan a receipt, the image is transmitted over an encrypted connection to Anthropic's API. Anthropic's AI processes the image to extract the merchant name, total amount, date, and line items. The image and extracted data are not stored on Anthropic's servers after processing is complete. Anthropic's privacy practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy.

Unreadable or invalid receipt images are discarded immediately. Receipt scanning requires an active internet connection.

5. AI Processing — Budget Setup

If you use the AI Budget Setup feature, the financial information you provide (income, city, household details, spending habits) is transmitted over an encrypted connection to Anthropic's API to generate a personalized budget recommendation. This information is not stored on Anthropic's servers after your budget is generated. The resulting budget is stored locally on your device and synced to your iCloud account. You are not required to use the AI Budget Setup feature — you can set your budget manually at any time.

6. Data Storage and Sync

Registered users: Your financial data is stored on your device using local storage and is automatically synced to your personal Apple iCloud account. This iCloud data is stored in your own Apple account — Receiptr does not have access to read, modify, or delete your iCloud data. Your data persists in iCloud even if you log out of Receiptr or delete the app. If you reinstall the app and log in, your data will be restored from iCloud.

Guest users: Guest data is stored only on your local device and is not synced to iCloud. If you delete the app, clear app storage, or lose your device, your guest data cannot be recovered. We strongly recommend creating a free account to protect your data.

We do not operate servers that store your financial transaction data. Your financial data lives in your iCloud account and on your device — not on our infrastructure.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third party for their own purposes. The only third party that receives any portion of your data is:

  • Anthropic — receipt images and budget setup information are sent to Anthropic's API for AI processing only. This data is not retained by Anthropic beyond the processing request.
  • Apple / iCloud — your data is synced through Apple's iCloud infrastructure under Apple's own privacy policies. Apple's privacy policy is available at apple.com/privacy.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics services, or tracking SDKs in Receiptr. There are no ads in Receiptr.

8. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and external services (Anthropic's API, iCloud) is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL encryption. Your iCloud data is protected by Apple's encryption and your Apple ID credentials. We recommend enabling Face ID or a passcode lock within the app for additional protection of your financial data.

While we take reasonable security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

9. Data Retention

Registered users: Your data is retained in your iCloud account until you explicitly delete it. Logging out of Receiptr clears your local device data only — your iCloud backup is preserved. To permanently delete all your Receiptr data from iCloud, go to iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Receiptr → Delete Data.

Guest users: Data is retained on your device until you delete the app, clear app data, or replace your device.

You can delete all local transaction data at any time through the app: Settings → Clear All Transactions.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: All your data is stored on your device and in your iCloud account — you have full access to it at all times within the app
  • Portability: You can export all your transaction data as a CSV file through Settings → Export to CSV
  • Deletion: You can delete all transaction data through Settings → Clear All Transactions, and delete your iCloud data through iPhone Settings as described above
  • Correction: You can edit any transaction, budget, or setting within the app at any time

To exercise any of these rights or submit a privacy request, contact us at eliasnemr7@gmail.com.

11. Children's Privacy

Receiptr is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through Receiptr, please contact us immediately at eliasnemr7@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

12. Canadian Privacy Law (PIPEDA)

For users in Canada, we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). We collect only the minimum personal information necessary to provide the service, with your knowledge and consent. You have the right to access your personal information and challenge its accuracy. For PIPEDA-related inquiries, contact us at eliasnemr7@gmail.com.

13. International Users

Receiptr is operated from Canada. If you are located outside Canada, your information may be processed in Canada and in the United States (where Anthropic operates). By using the app, you consent to this transfer. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable privacy laws.

We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, through an in-app notification. Your continued use of Receiptr after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
