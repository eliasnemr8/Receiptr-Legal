Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Summary: Receiptr uses Google Firebase to securely store your financial data and sync it across your devices. We also use Apple iCloud as an additional backup. We do not sell your data, we do not advertise to you, and we do not share your financial data with anyone except the services listed in this policy. Receipt images and budget setup information are processed by Anthropic's AI and are not retained after processing.

1. Who We Are

Receiptr ("we", "us", "our") is a personal expense tracking application developed and operated as an independent app. For privacy inquiries, contact us at hello@receiptrapp.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account information (registered users only):

  • Name and email address provided at signup
  • Account creation date and time
  • A unique Firebase account ID (UID) assigned at registration

Financial data you enter or scan:

  • Merchant names, transaction amounts, dates, and categories
  • Receipt images you choose to scan
  • Individual line items from receipts
  • Transaction notes you add

Budget and savings information:

  • Budget limits you set per category
  • Savings goals, target amounts, and target dates
  • Recurring transaction schedules you configure
  • Budget reset settings and preferences

AI Budget Setup information (if you use this feature):

  • Monthly income range
  • City and country of residence
  • Household size
  • Housing situation and estimated costs
  • Transportation method and estimated costs
  • Dining habits
  • Whether you have children or pets and estimated costs
  • Savings goals

App preferences:

  • Currency selection
  • Custom categories you create
  • Notification and budget reset settings
  • Face ID / biometric lock preference

What we do NOT collect:

  • Bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or any payment credentials
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Browsing history or behavior outside the app
  • Contacts or other device data

3. How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used solely to provide the Receiptr service to you:

  • To authenticate your account securely via Firebase Authentication
  • To display your transaction history, spending summaries, and budget progress
  • To sync your data across your devices via Firebase Firestore and Apple iCloud
  • To process receipt images through AI to extract transaction details
  • To generate a personalized budget through AI based on your financial situation
  • To send transactional emails such as email verification and password reset
  • To deliver local budget alert notifications on your device
  • To allow you to export your data as a CSV file

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the app's features to you.

4. AI Processing — Receipt Scanning

When you scan a receipt, the image is transmitted over an encrypted connection to Anthropic's API. Anthropic's AI processes the image to extract the merchant name, total amount, date, and line items. The image and extracted data are not stored on Anthropic's servers after processing is complete. Anthropic's privacy practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy.

Unreadable or invalid receipt images are discarded immediately. Receipt scanning requires an active internet connection. Free accounts include 5 AI receipt scans. Receiptr Pro includes unlimited scanning.

5. AI Processing — Budget Setup

If you use the AI Budget Setup feature, the financial information you provide (income, city, household details, spending habits) is transmitted over an encrypted connection to Anthropic's API to generate a personalized budget recommendation. This information is not stored on Anthropic's servers after your budget is generated. The resulting budget is stored locally on your device, synced to Firebase Firestore, and backed up to your iCloud account. You are not required to use the AI Budget Setup feature — you can set your budget manually at any time.

6. Data Storage and Sync

Registered users: Your financial data is stored in three places:

  • Google Firebase Firestore — your transaction data, budgets, savings goals, and app settings are securely stored in Google Firebase's cloud database, tied to your unique Firebase account ID. This allows your data to sync across any device you log into. Firebase data is encrypted at rest and in transit by Google.
  • Your device — data is also cached locally for fast, offline access.
  • Apple iCloud — an additional backup of your data is stored in your personal iCloud account on iOS devices, providing an extra layer of redundancy.

Your data in Firebase is associated with your unique account ID only. Firestore security rules ensure that only you can read or write your own data — no other user or third party can access your financial information.

Guest users: Guest data is stored only on your local device and is not synced to Firebase or iCloud. If you delete the app, clear app storage, or lose your device, your guest data cannot be recovered. We strongly recommend creating a free account to protect your data.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third party for their own purposes. The third-party services that receive portions of your data as part of providing the Receiptr service are:

  • Google Firebase — your account authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication, and your app data (transactions, budgets, savings, preferences) is stored in Firebase Firestore. Firebase is operated by Google LLC and is subject to Google's Firebase Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy. Google does not use your Receiptr data for advertising purposes.
  • Anthropic — receipt images and budget setup information are sent to Anthropic's API for AI processing only. This data is not retained by Anthropic beyond the processing request. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple iCloud — your data is additionally backed up through Apple's iCloud infrastructure under your personal Apple account. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Resend — transactional emails such as email address verification and password reset emails are sent through Resend's email delivery infrastructure from hello@receiptrapp.com. Resend only receives your email address for the purpose of delivery and does not have access to your financial data. Subject to Resend's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — subscription payments for Receiptr Pro are processed by Apple. Apple receives payment information in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not receive or store your payment details.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics services, or tracking SDKs in Receiptr. There are no ads in Receiptr.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL) between the app and all external services
  • Firebase Authentication for secure account access with email verification
  • Firestore security rules that restrict data access to the account owner only
  • Google Firebase's encryption of data at rest and in transit
  • Apple's encryption for iCloud backups

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law. We recommend enabling Face ID or a passcode lock within the app for additional protection.

9. Data Retention

Registered users: Your data is retained in Firebase Firestore and your iCloud account until you explicitly request deletion. Logging out of Receiptr clears your local device cache only — your Firebase and iCloud data is preserved so you can restore it on any device by logging back in. To request permanent deletion of all your data from Firebase Firestore, contact us at hello@receiptrapp.com. To delete your iCloud backup, go to iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Receiptr → Delete Data.

Guest users: Data is retained on your local device until you delete the app, clear app data, or replace your device. Guest data is not backed up anywhere and cannot be recovered once lost.

You can delete all local transaction data at any time through the app: Settings → Clear All Transactions.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Your data is accessible at all times within the app. You can view all your transactions, budgets, and settings directly.
  • Portability: You can export all your transaction data as a CSV file through Settings → Export to CSV.
  • Deletion: You can delete all transaction data through Settings → Clear All Transactions. For full account and Firebase data deletion, contact us at hello@receiptrapp.com.
  • Correction: You can edit any transaction, budget, or setting within the app at any time.

To exercise any of these rights or submit a privacy request, contact us at hello@receiptrapp.com.

11. Children's Privacy

Receiptr is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through Receiptr, please contact us immediately at hello@receiptrapp.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

12. Canadian Privacy Law (PIPEDA)

For users in Canada, we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). We collect only the minimum personal information necessary to provide the service, with your knowledge and consent. You have the right to access your personal information and challenge its accuracy. For PIPEDA-related inquiries, contact us at hello@receiptrapp.com.

13. International Users

Receiptr is operated from Canada. If you are located outside Canada, your information may be processed in Canada and in the United States, where Anthropic, Google Firebase, and Resend operate their servers. By using the app, you consent to this transfer. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable privacy laws.
